Summary

About the movie

Descendants 3 is an American musical fantasy television film, which is the third installment in the Descendants series, following Descendants and Descendants 2. The film premiered on Disney Channel on August 2, 2019, and in other parts of the world on October 12, 2019.

Plot overview

The teenagers of Disney's most infamous villains return to the Isle of the Lost to recruit a new batch of villainous offspring to join them at Auradon Prep.
